## WEDGE LANGUAGE

```
THE WEDGE: The unfulfilled loop that makes you return.

RIGHT WAY TO SAY IT:
  "You left in the middle of something. The system remembered."
  "Your lab is waiting."
  "Level 2 is one session away."

WRONG WAY TO SAY IT:
  "You have notifications."
  "You have items to complete."
  "Don't forget your work."
```

The wedge is pull, not push. Never guilt. Never notification pressure.
The system creates incompleteness — the user resolves it on their own terms.
