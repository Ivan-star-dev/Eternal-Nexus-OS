## ABOUT — LONG VERSION

I am a founder, principal architect, system builder and inventor from Cape Verde, now based in the Netherlands.

I think top-down, design in systems, and build organisms instead of disconnected products. My work lives where AI, infrastructure, defense, continuity, regeneration and human systems meet.

My path was not conventional. I come from years of structural friction, migration pressure, injustice, physical pain and survival across Cabo Verde, Portugal and the Netherlands. That pressure did not erase me — it revealed me. It made one thing clear: my gift is building systems.

Over the years, I have led people under pressure, helped immigrants navigate hostile systems, built businesses in difficult conditions, and learned to translate chaos into structure. I care deeply about systems that do not just function, but protect, restore and scale with integrity.

Today, I am building Ruberra Corp and the Eternal Nexus ecosystem: a long-range architecture for sovereign systems where product, system, defense, governance, memory and identity operate as one living organism.

I am especially interested in:
- AI systems and coordination
- Infrastructure and environmental regeneration
- Sovereign digital architecture
- System design and continuity
- Human-centered operating models
- Future-ready products with real depth

My principles are simple:
- Retention over empty reinvention
- Organism over fragment
- Identity over drift
- Function over noise
- Human value above everything

I am always drawn to the biggest problems in the room — the ones that need architecture, vision and courage, not superficial fixes.
